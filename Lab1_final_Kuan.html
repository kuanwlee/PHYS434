When integrating from -infinity to a point that is -0.1497 away from the mean, we get a probability of 0.4405. It means that the probability of getting a signal that is stronger than the signal at -0.1497 sigma away from the mean is (1-0.4405) = 0.5596
This suggests that the probability that a background give me a reading that is as or more signal like is 0.5596.
